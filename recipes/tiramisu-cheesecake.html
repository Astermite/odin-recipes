Tiramisu Cheesecake

Description

Experience the best of both worlds with Tiramisu Cheesecake, a luscious fusion of two beloved desserts. This indulgent creation combines the creamy richness of cheesecake with the irresistible flavors of tiramisu. Picture velvety layers of coffee-infused cream cheese, nestled between delicate ladyfinger cookies soaked in espresso. Topped with a dusting of cocoa powder, every forkful is a decadent symphony of textures and tastes, marrying the classic charm of tiramisu with the velvety luxury of cheesecake.

Ingredients

    For the Cheesecake Base:

  • 11/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup mented butter
  • 2 tablespoons sugar

    • For the Cheesecake Filling:

  • 24 ounces cream cheese,softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup all purpose flour

    • For the Tiramisu Layer:

  • 1 cup strong brewed coffee,cooled
  • 2 tablespoons coffee liqueur(optional)
  • 24 ladyfinger cookies

    • For the Topping:

  • Cocoa poeder for dusting

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F(163°C) and grease a 9-inch springform pan.
  2. In a bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs, mented butter, and sugar for the cheesecake base. Press the mixture into the bottom of the prepared pan.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after eacj addition. Stir in vanilla extract anf flour.
  4. Pour the cream cheese mixture over the graham cracker crust, spreading it evenly.
  5. In a shallow dish, combine brewed coffee and coffee liqueur. Dip each ladyfinger into the coffee mixture, ensuring they are soaked but not overly soggy. Arrange a layer of soaked ladyfingers over the cream cheese filling.
  6. Repeat the process with another layer of cream cheese filling and soaked ladyfingers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of cream cheese on top.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 55-60 minutes or until the center is set.
  8. Allow the cheesecake to cool completely, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  9. Before serving, dust the top of the cheesecake with cocoa powder for the perfect finishing touch.
  10. Slice and savor the heavenly marriage of cheesecake and tiramisu in every delicious bite of Tiramisu Cheesecake. Enjoy!
