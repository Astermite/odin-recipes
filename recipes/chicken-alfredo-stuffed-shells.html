Chicken alfredo stuffed shells

Description

Indulge in the savory elegance of Chicken Alfredo Stuffed Shells, a culinary masterpiece that seamlessly marries the richness of Alfredo sauce with the comforting goodness of shredded chicken and tender spinach. Each pasta shell cradles a sumptuous filling, creating a harmonious blend of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds. Baked to golden perfection, this dish is a delightful combination of creamy, cheesy, and hearty goodness.

Ingredients

    For the filling:

  • 1 pound jumbo pasta shells
  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup chopped fresh spinach
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper for taste

    • For the Alfredo Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 2 cups grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder

    • For Garnish:

  • Chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Cook the jumbo pasta shells according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  3. In a large bowl, combine shredded chicken, ricotta cheese, chopped spinach, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, egg, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  4. Stuff each cooked pasta shell with the chicken and cheese mixture and place them in a baking dish.
  5. In a saucepan, melt butter for the Alfredo sauce over medium heat. Add heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Stir continuously until the sauce thickens.
  6. Pour the Alfredo sauce over the stuffed shells, ensuring they are well-coated.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until the top is golden and bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven, garnish with chopped fresh parsley, and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.
  9. Serve these delectable Chicken Alfredo Stuffed Shells hot, and delight in the harmonious blend of creamy textures and savory flavors. Enjoy!
